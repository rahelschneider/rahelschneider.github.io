Hi, I'am Rahel! I'm a student of Human Computer Interaction (B.Sc.) in the 6th semester. I like to get in contact with users, discover their needs and present them with an ideal solution. My special interests are Sustainable UX and Inclusive Design.

Work experience

In addition to my university projects, I worked as a working student at SSI Schäfer for one year. During this year I created templates for describing internal UI components and programmed dashboard extensions for WAMAS® Lighthouse. Before my time at SSI Schäfer I worked as a tutor for the module "Basics of Programming" at the University of Würzburg . There I taught the basics of the programming languages Java and C.
